# Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how LogoFlow Marquee ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and stores information when merchants install or use the LogoFlow Marquee Shopify app.

## 1. Information We Collect

When you install or use the app, we may collect and process the following information:

- Shop information, including your `myshopify.com` shop domain
- Shopify app session data and authentication/session tokens
- Subscription status and billing status information returned by Shopify
- App configuration data needed to provide the app's functionality
- Theme-extension related settings used to render the logo marquee experience
- Technical information such as request metadata, error logs, and basic diagnostic information

We do not intentionally collect customer payment card data through the app. Billing and payment processing for managed pricing are handled by Shopify.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Authenticate merchants and maintain secure app sessions
- Determine whether an active paid plan exists for the shop
- Provide access to the app dashboard and theme extension features
- Save and load app settings required for app functionality
- Troubleshoot errors, maintain security, and improve app stability
- Comply with legal obligations and Shopify platform requirements

## 3. Shopify Billing

LogoFlow Marquee uses Shopify managed pricing. Subscription approval, recurring billing, and billing-related payment handling are managed by Shopify. We may receive limited billing status information from Shopify, such as whether a merchant has an active subscription to the app.

## 4. Data Storage

Depending on the environment, app session and operational data may be stored using secure application storage mechanisms such as MongoDB or SQLite. In production, session data may be stored in MongoDB to support merchant authentication and app operation.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell merchant personal information.

We may share information only as necessary with:

- Shopify, to authenticate the app, verify shop access, and determine subscription status
- Service providers or infrastructure providers that help us host, secure, monitor, or operate the app
- Legal authorities where required by law, regulation, subpoena, or other lawful request

## 6. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as necessary to operate the app, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and maintain security. Some session or log data may remain for a limited period after app uninstall for operational or legal reasons, unless deleted earlier.

## 7. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information associated with the app. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. Merchant Rights

If you are a merchant and would like to request access to, correction of, or deletion of information associated with your use of the app, you may contact us using the contact information below. We may need to verify your identity and shop ownership before processing certain requests.

## 9. Third-Party Services

The app operates on the Shopify platform and may rely on third-party infrastructure or hosting providers. Your use of Shopify and other third-party services is also subject to their respective terms and privacy policies.

## 10. Children

The app is intended for business use by Shopify merchants and is not directed to children.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect operational, legal, or regulatory changes. When we do, we will update the effective date above.
